Burger

Description

This recipe is a very delicious burger. You can add all kinds of toppings you like! This recipe only incorporates the basics

Ingredients

  • burger buns
  • patty
  • tomato
  • lettuce
  • tomatoes
  • your favorite sauce

Steps

  1. Fry your patties in a pan with oil at low heat
  2. While your patties are frying, wash and cut your toppings
  3. Warm up your burger buns
  4. Put the ingredients together as you like and enjoy!
